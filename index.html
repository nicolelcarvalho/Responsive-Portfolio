About Me

I am an aspiring web developer who isn't afraid of a challenge. Unsolved problems motivate me and the continuous learning that comes with coding excites me. My need to achieve perfection is the backbone to my strong attention to detail. I am driven to excel both independently as well as in teams. I exude a glass-half-full attitude and truly believe you can do anything you put your mind to.

I have a B.S. in Marketing from Philadalphia University as well as five years of work experience in the fields of sales, management and public relations for a luxury department store. Currently, I am a full-time student pursuing Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. When I'm not coding, you can find me baking, building and designing some pretty awesome cakes.
